# How to Sync Your Workout Calendar

A simple guide to exporting your workout schedule to your favorite calendar app.

 ---

## Quick Start

1. Open your 5/3/1 cycle in the app
2. Go to the **Calendar** tab
3. Click the **üìÖ Export** button
4. Choose your preferred calendar platform

That's it! Your workouts will be added to your calendar.

## What Gets Added to Your Calendar?

Each workout event includes:

- **Title**: Exercise name and workout day (e.g., "Squat - Week 1 Day 1")
- **Time**: Your scheduled workout time
- **Duration**: 1 hour (you can adjust this in your calendar app)
- **Details**:
  - Main sets with weights and reps
  - Warmup sets
  - Assistance exercises
  - AMRAP indicators
- **Location**: Your training cycle name

## Tips & Tricks

### üí° Adding Multiple Workouts

- **Google/Outlook Calendar**: Add workouts one at a time using the direct buttons
- **Apple Calendar & Others**: Download the ICS file to add all workouts at once

### üí° Setting Reminders

After adding workouts to your calendar:

1. Open the event in your calendar app
2. Add a reminder (e.g., 30 minutes before)
3. Your calendar app will notify you before your workout

### üí° Updating Workouts

If you change your workout schedule:

1. Delete the old events from your calendar
2. Export the updated schedule again
3. Import the new events

### üí° Sharing with Your Trainer

Want to share your workout schedule?

1. Export to ICS file
2. Email the ICS file to your trainer
3. They can import it into their calendar

## Troubleshooting

### ‚ùì File Won't Open

**Solution**: Make sure you have a calendar app installed. Right-click the file and choose "Open with" ‚Üí [Your Calendar App]

### ‚ùì Nothing Happens When I Click Google/Outlook

**Solution**: Check if pop-ups are blocked in your browser. Allow pop-ups for this site, or use the ICS download option instead.

### ‚ùì No Workouts to Export

**Solution**: Make sure you have:

1. Created a 5/3/1 cycle
2. Scheduled workouts using the Schedule Manager
3. Workouts showing in your Calendar view

### ‚ùì Strange Characters in Calendar

**Solution**: This is rare but can happen with older calendar apps. Try using a different calendar app or update your current one.
